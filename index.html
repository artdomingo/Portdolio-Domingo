Recent Projects

Mobile App

SKINSCAN

an intelligent mobile and web-based application designed to analyze skin conditions through image processing and artificial intelligence. By allowing users to upload or capture photos of affected skin areas, SKINSCAN automatically detects, identifies, and provides preliminary diagnoses of common dermatological issues such as psoriasis and livedo reticularis. The platform also offers guided questionnaires to gather user symptoms and delivers helpful information to support early awareness and preventive care.

Web App

Network company

web application designed to support companies that provide networking services such as infrastructure setup, maintenance, and technical support. The system enables efficient management of client records, network installation projects, service requests, and technician scheduling. It offers real-time monitoring of network devices, logs service performance, and tracks issue resolution history.

Billing system

Food bussines

is a desktop-based application designed to simplify and automate the billing process in restaurants, cafés, and other food establishments. Developed with C# and connected to a MySQL database, it allows staff to manage menu items, process customer orders, compute totals including taxes and discounts, and generate official receipts. The system also supports daily sales tracking, inventory management, and report generation, ensuring efficient and accurate transaction handling tailored for food service operations.
